# **App Name**: Sandoval Strategics

## Core Features:

- Strategic Vision & Value Proposition: A compelling hero section that articulates Karen's unique blend of design, technical viability, and business strategy to optimize scalable digital ecosystems, ensuring immediate impact for potential clients.
- Integrated Digital Competencies: Showcase Karen's expertise across three strategic pillars: Product Strategy & UX, Visual Development & Front-end, and Business Optimization (SEO/QA/Management) to provide a holistic view of capabilities.
- Impact-Driven Project Showcase: Detailed presentation of three cornerstone projects (Aviatur, Bit-C, Kaput), utilizing the STAR methodology to highlight situations, tasks, actions, and quantifiable results, demonstrating tangible business value.
- Distinguished Career Path: A concise and authoritative overview of professional experience at Aecsa and Aviatur, emphasizing leadership in QA validation and talent development, reinforcing a track record of excellence.
- Connect & Collaborate Gateway: A streamlined contact mechanism allowing potential clients to initiate collaboration and inquire about Karen's services, fostering direct engagement.
- AI-Powered Strategy Architect: An interactive tool offering preliminary content optimization or SEO brief recommendations for user-submitted topics or URLs, demonstrating a fraction of Karen's strategic prowess and serving as a lead generation asset.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color Scheme: Dark. The palette conveys professionalism, strategic depth, and digital sophistication. The background is a very dark blue-gray (#111418), offering a calm yet authoritative base.
- Primary Color: A deep, rich indigo (#486DDB) provides a sense of reliability and strategic vision. It stands out clearly against the dark background for headlines and key information.
- Accent Color: A vibrant aqua-cyan (#4DD7F3), used sparingly for interactive elements and highlights, adds a modern digital flair and guides user attention.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its clean, objective, and modern aesthetic. This ensures high legibility and conveys a professional, tech-oriented authority suitable for both headlines and detailed content.
- Spacious, clean layouts with strong visual hierarchy. Generous use of whitespace ensures content clarity and a sophisticated, unencumbered user experience.
- Minimalist, line-based icons in the accent color to maintain a sleek, professional, and digital aesthetic without distracting from core content.
- Subtle, deliberate transitions and hover effects to guide user interaction and enhance the feeling of a polished, high-performance digital product.
